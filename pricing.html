Pricing Questions

Common questions about our pricing, policies, and payment options.

Several factors influence your final price:

  • Treatment area size: Larger areas require more product or time, which affects cost.
  • Your goals: More dramatic changes may require more sessions or product.
  • Product amount: For injectables, the number of units or syringes needed.
  • Number of sessions: Some treatments require multiple visits for best results.
  • Membership status: Our members receive 10-20% off all treatments.

We'll give you an exact quote during your free consultation, so you know the price before committing.
