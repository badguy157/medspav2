Questions people ask

Here are answers to the most common questions.

Does it hurt?

Most treatments feel like a small pinch. We use numbing cream to help. Most people say it's easier than they expected.

How long until I see results?

It depends on the treatment. Some work right away. Others take 1-2 weeks to show full results. We'll tell you what to expect.

Will I need time to recover?

Most treatments need little or no downtime. You can usually go back to work the same day. We'll let you know if you need rest.

Will people notice I had something done?

Our goal is natural results. People might say you look rested or refreshed. But they won't know you had a treatment.

How much does it cost?

Prices vary by treatment. Check our pricing page for ranges. We'll give you exact costs in your consultation.
