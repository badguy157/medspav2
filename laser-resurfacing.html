Timeline & results

What to expect after your laser resurfacing treatment

Day 1: Right after treatment

You may see tiny bumps where we injected, but they fade within an hour. You can return to work and normal activities immediately. Avoid lying down for 4 hours and don't exercise heavily for 24 hours.

Days 3-7: Results begin

You'll start to notice your wrinkles smoothing out. The full effect develops gradually over this week.

Week 2: Full results

Your results are now complete. You'll see smoother skin and a more refreshed appearance.

Months 3-4: Maintenance

Results typically last 3-4 months. Book your next appointment when you notice lines starting to return.

Frequently asked questions

Does Botox hurt?

Most people describe it as a tiny pinch. The needle is very small and the injections are quick. If you're nervous, we can apply a numbing cream first.

Will I look frozen or fake?

Not when done correctly. Our goal is natural results‚Äîyou'll still be able to make expressions, just with fewer wrinkles. We use conservative doses to avoid an overdone look.

How long does Botox last?

Results typically last 3-4 months. First-time patients sometimes see results fade a bit sooner, around 2-3 months. With regular treatments, results may last longer.

Can I combine Botox with other treatments?

Yes! Botox works well with fillers, facials, and other skin treatments. We can create a comprehensive plan during your consultation.

What's the difference between Botox and fillers?

Botox relaxes muscles to smooth wrinkles caused by expressions (like frown lines). Fillers add volume to areas that have lost fullness (like lips or cheeks). Many people benefit from both.
